FireHound

A fully-customized AOSP ROM with lots of useful features

Who We Are

FireHound is a Custom ROM developed with a major focus on system stability and useful features. All this without compromising on the pure Android experience.



Up-to-date Security Patches

We merge latest android revisions as soon as Google releases them and we are satisfied that they do not cause unstability

Ashes

The core customization's hub written from scratch built-in to the the Settings app to provide you with a range of customizations

Essentials

Our default app written from scratch used for Over-The-Air updates and applying our Wallpapers

Bloat Free

We have removed the excess un-needed heavy apps and replaced some with a lighter version to provide a lag free experience

Open Source

Every part of our project is Open Source and can be found on the Github organization.

Smoothness

The ROM is tweaked and optimized to provide you with a smooth and lag free experience
